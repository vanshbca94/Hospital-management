About Our Project

The Hospital Management System (HMS) is a modern web-based platform designed to simplify hospital operations. It integrates appointment booking, doctor attendance tracking, and administrative tools into a seamless digital experience.

✨ Appointment Booking

Patients can easily book and manage appointments online.

🩺 Doctor Attendance

Doctors can log attendance and track schedules effectively.

⚡ Admin Dashboard

Admin controls for managing doctors, patients, and hospital resources.
